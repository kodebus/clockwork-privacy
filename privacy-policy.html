⏱️ Clockwork - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

Clockwork is a Chrome extension that automatically tracks time spent on websites. We take your privacy seriously.

✅ Your data stays 100% local in your browser
✅ Nothing is sent to external servers
✅ No account required
✅ No tracking or analytics

What Data We Collect

When you use Clockwork, the extension collects:

  • Website URLs - To identify which sites you visit (e.g., github.com, gmail.com)
  • Page Titles - To determine what you're working on
  • Time Spent - Duration on each website
  • Activity Categories - Automated classification (e.g., "Coding", "Email")

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally in your Chrome browser using Chrome's secure storage API.

  • ✅ Data never leaves your device
  • ✅ No external servers
  • ✅ No cloud storage
  • ✅ No third-party access

How We Use Your Data

Your tracked data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your time tracking dashboard
  • Generate activity summaries
  • Create CSV export files (locally on your device)

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Send your data to any servers
  • ❌ Share data with third parties
  • ❌ Use data for advertising
  • ❌ Track your behavior outside the extension

Data Security

  • All data stored using Chrome's encrypted storage
  • Only accessible by you in your browser
  • Protected by Chrome's security model

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View: All data is visible in the extension dashboard
  • Export: Download your data anytime via CSV export
  • Delete: Uninstall the extension to delete all data immediately

Third-Party Services

Clockwork does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

No cookies. No analytics. No tracking.

Children's Privacy

Clockwork is intended for adults and professional use. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
